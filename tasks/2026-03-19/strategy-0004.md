## What This Does (Plain English)
This is an automated helper that sets up a travel plan for someone named **Shuo** visiting the Champaign-Urbana, Illinois area from **March 28–31, 2026**.
---
## Overall Goal
It creates **four day-by-day lists** in Google Maps — one for each day of the trip — so Shuo can easily navigate to each place without manually searching for addresses.
---
## Main Steps (In Order)
1. **Builds four daily lists**, each containing the places Shuo plans to visit that day (like restaurants, hotels, apartments, and landmarks).
2. **Saves each list to Google Maps** automatically, one by one.
3. **Records the results** — whether each list was created successfully and how many places were added.
4. **Writes a summary** of what was done to a notes file on the computer for future reference.
---
## What Each Day Covers
- **Saturday, Mar 28** — Arrival day: lunch, campus sightseeing with Ashley, dinner, and hotel check-in.
- **Sunday, Mar 29** — Check into a new hotel, visit car dealerships, tour university housing, and dinner.
- **Monday, Mar 30** — Full day of apartment and housing tours, with several Chinese restaurant options for dinner.
- **Tuesday, Mar 31** — Check out, drive to Chicago O'Hare Airport to return the rental car, and check into an airport hotel.
---
## What Gets Saved
- The **name of each list** and whether it was created successfully.
- A **final summary** is appended to a notes file on the computer, recording the date and what was accomplished.
