## What This Does (In Plain Terms)
Think of this like a shopping helper that goes to Amazon for you and finds the best deal on CoQ10 vitamins.
## Overall Goal
Find up to 10 CoQ10 vitamin supplements on Amazon, then figure out which one gives you the most "bang for your buck" — meaning the lowest cost per pill.
## Main Steps (In Order)
1. **Searches Amazon** for "CoQ10 supplement" and grabs up to 10 results
2. **Reads each product's details** — the name, price, customer rating, pill strength (in milligrams), and how many pills are in the bottle
3. **Calculates the cost per pill** by dividing the total price by the number of pills
4. **Sorts the results** from cheapest-per-pill to most expensive (best deal first)
5. **Saves the findings** to a notes file, including a table of all products and a callout for the single best value
## Data Collected
- Product name
- Price
- Customer rating
- Pill strength (mg)
- Number of pills in the package
- Cost per pill (the key comparison number)
