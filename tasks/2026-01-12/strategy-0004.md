## What This Does
This is an automated helper that organizes a family trip to Anchorage, Alaska and shares the plan with someone.

  - The Voyager Inn (the hotel chosen because it's closest to everything)
  - Four museums to visit during the trip

### 4. **Shares the Plan**

- Uses Microsoft Teams (a messaging app) to deliver it
